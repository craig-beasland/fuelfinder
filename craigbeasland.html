The Fuel Finder application was created by Craig Beasland using the RSS capabilities available on the Consumer Protection fuelwatch website. The website is available at http://www.fuelwatch.wa.gov.au. This project was designed because I had a personal need and also as a tool to learn web/html/mobile application development.

During the day I work at Hotmix Group. My day to day role involves assisting various clients in the WA business community with understnading the data that they have available (or unavailable) to them.

Hotmix Group can be contacted to assist with all your day-to-day Information & Technology requirements.

I can be contacted via email to craig@hotmix.com.au if you have any comments or suggestions on this app or other apps that might be useful.
